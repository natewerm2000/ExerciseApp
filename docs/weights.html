Weights

Dumbbells
Dumbbells, the steryotypical free weight that most people think of when they hear the word freeweights. When it comes to resistance/strength training, dumbbells are an important component in many workouts. The uses of dumbbells are almost infinite, ranging from shoulder raises, chest flyers, and bicep curls. Dumbbells can also add resistance to normal commpound movement workouts, like squats and chest presses.

Workouts good for dumbbells:

Goblet Squats

Bench Press

Shoulder Press

Chest/Rear Deltoid Flys

and many more!

Weight Plates
If you want to add weight to your barbells to get stronger, weight plates are the way to go. Weight plates can simply be used by sliding it on your barbell set to add more weight, or they can even be used as compound free weights.

These freeweight exercises include:

Lateral Raise

Front Shoulder Raise

Plate Push-Up

Overhead Press

Side Bench

Squat Reach
